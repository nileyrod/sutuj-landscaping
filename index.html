About Sutuj Landscaping

Since 2013, Sutuj Landscaping has been proudly serving families and businesses throughout Midlothian, Chesterfield, and the surrounding communities. What started as a passion for creating beautiful outdoor spaces has grown into a trusted local business known for quality craftsmanship and attention to detail.

We're a family-owned company that treats every property like our own. Whether you need regular lawn care to keep things looking sharp or a complete hardscape transformation with custom patios and outdoor living spaces, our experienced team delivers results you'll love.

  • Over a decade of local experience
  • Custom designs tailored to your yard
  • Clean, respectful crew on every job
  • Quality materials and lasting craftsmanship
